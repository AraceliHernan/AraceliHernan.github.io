Welcome to my personal website.

My name is Araceli Hernández. I am a professor at the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid, and I teach in the field of power systems.

Most of my research has focused on the study of power quality in electrical systems. I also analyze the challenges arising from the conversion of generation based on electronic converters in distribution systems.

I created this page to make my information and some of the work I produce accessible, but I am just getting started!

So please be patient, as I will upload everything gradually.

Thank you for visiting!
